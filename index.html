First National Conference of Innovation
in Data Engineering and AI Science

Exchange to Innovate

17 - 18 Juin 2025 at University of Science and Technology Of Oran Mohamed Boudiaf USTO MB

 

Welcome to IDEAS 2025!
The IDEAS 2025 conference is an Hybrid major scientific event focused on the latest advancements in data engineering and artificial intelligence. It will bring together researchers, professionals, and students to explore new perspectives and applications of cutting-edge technologies in these fields.

This event offers a unique platform to discuss current challenges, innovative solutions, and emerging trends in intelligent systems, data science, and AI.

 

Why Attend IDEAS 2025?
The goal of this conference is to bring together leaders in the field of AI and data engineering to:

  • Explore the latest trends and innovations in data technologies and AI.

  • Share research and practical solutions to current challenges in the field.

  • Establish partnerships between researchers, professionals, and students for a better digital future.

We invite you to attend the conference, submit your research, and work with us to influence technology in the future!

 

CAll For Papers

The Organizing Committee of the National Conference on Innovation in Data Engineering and AI Science (IDEAS 2025) is delighted to invite researchers, practitioners, and academics to submit their original contributions to this exciting event.

We look forward to your submissions and hope to welcome you to IDEAS 2025 for a memorable and innovative event!

 

DEADLINES

  • Submission Deadline: April 10, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: May 20, 2025
  • Camera-Ready Submission: June 1, 2025
  • Conference Dates: June 17-18, 2025

SUBMISSION

  • Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research papers. Submissions must adhere to the following guidelines:

      • Short Papers: 2-3 pages, including references.
      • Long Papers: 6-8 pages, including references.
  • All papers will undergo a double-blind peer-review process.

 

PUBLICATION

Accepted papers will be included in the conference proceedings and may be considered for publication in a special issue of COST and AJRT Journals.

TOPICS

The IDEAS 2025 conference highlights six main axes, reflecting its commitment to innovation in data engineering and artificial intelligence. Relevant topics include, but are not limited to:  

Big Data Analytics and Visualization

  • - Big Data Frameworks, Data Visualization Techniques, Real-Time Analytics, Predictive Analytics Models

  • - Business Intelligence Integration, Scalable Data Storage, IoT Data Analytics.

  • - Ethical Data Challenges, Healthcare Data Insights, Emerging Analytics Trends.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

  • - Deep Learning Models, Reinforcement Learning Techniques, Natural Language Processing

    • - AI in Healthcare, Computer Vision Applications, Explainable AI Methods

    - Ethical AI Challenges, Transfer Learning Approaches, Federated Learning Systems, AI for Cybersecurity

Data Engineering and Intelligent Systems

  • - Data Quality Management, Edge Computing Frameworks, Smart Decision Systems.

  • - Data Pipeline Design, Distributed Data Systems, Cloud Data Integration, Intelligent System Architectures

  • - Automated Data Processing, Knowledge Representation Models, Intelligent IoT Systems.

AI Applications in Industry and Healthcare

  • - AI in Manufacturing, Predictive Maintenance Models, Smart Supply Chains, AI in Finance.

  • - Personalized Healthcare Solutions, Medical Imaging Analysis, Drug Discovery Automation.

  • - AI for Remote Monitoring, Healthcare Chatbots Development, Robotics in Surgery.

Ethics, Privacy, and Security in AI and Data Science

  • - AI Ethical Frameworks, Bias in Algorithms, Data Privacy Laws, Secure Data Sharing.

  • - AI in Cybersecurity, Explainability in AI, Responsible Data Usage.

  • - Adversarial AI Attacks, Trustworthy AI Systems, Privacy-Preserving Models.

Emerging Trends in AI and Data Science

  • - Quantum Machine Learning, Neuromorphic Computing, AI-Driven Automation, Synthetic Data Generation.

  • - Autonomous Systems Evolution, Edge AI Integration, Human-AI Collaboration.

  • - AI in Blockchain, Multimodal AI Models, Sustainable AI Practices.

AUTHOR GUIDELINES

 

Submission Process

  • Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research papers. Submissions must adhere to the following guidelines:

      • Short Papers: 2-3 pages, including references.
      • Long Papers: 6-8 pages, including references.

    • Submissions must be in English or Arabic or French and strictly follow the attached IEEE template (double-column format) and must be via Submission Platform

    All submissions will undergo a rigorous blind peer-review process to ensure high-quality contributions to the conference.


Ethical Standards

By submitting a paper, authors agree to the following:

  • The submission is original and has not been published or submitted elsewhere.
  • The paper complies with ethical research standards, including appropriate citations and credit for previous work.
  • Authors must disclose any potential conflicts of interest.

Late submissions or incomplete papers will not be reviewed.

Camera-Ready Submission

Once a paper is accepted, authors will be required to:

  • Include author names and affiliations in the final version.
  • Address all reviewer comments in the revised manuscript.
  • Ensure the final paper adheres to IEEE formatting guidelines.
  • Register for the conference to present the paper.

Presentation at the Conference

At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the conference and present their work during the scheduled session. Failure to present may result in the paper being excluded from the conference proceedings.

Proceedings and Special Issue

Accepted and presented papers will be included in the conference proceedings and may be considered for publication in a special issue of a reputable journal. Details will be provided during the conference.
